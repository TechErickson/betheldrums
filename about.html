Who We Are

The Heartbeat of Bethel Drums

Bethel Drums is a premier percussion and entertainment company based in Kenya. We were founded on the belief that rhythm is a universal language — one that connects people, builds communities, and transforms ordinary moments into unforgettable experiences.

From intimate music lessons that nurture the next generation of drummers, to electrifying corporate performances that unite entire organisations — Bethel Drums shows up, every time, with passion and precision.

Our team of professional drummers and music educators bring creativity, energy, and expertise to everything we do. Whether we're crafting a percussive soundscape for a brand campaign or making a birthday unforgettable, we pour our hearts into every beat.
